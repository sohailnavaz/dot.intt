About Us


We are a Professional Organization with dedicated team of qualified Insurance Professionals, Risk Engineers, Chartered Accountants and specialized Management Graduates to service various Insurance and Risk Management requirements of clients spread across the country. We are located in cities across India, specialized in evolving Insurance Portfolio Management Solutions for the corporate sector across the spectrum of industries with above 200 satisfied Clients.

Securisk is one of the country's fast expanding risk management and insurance Services Company, staffed by professionals who are risk experts, operating out of major cities in India. We provide risk management and insurance broking services to business, professional and public entity organizations. We provide the total range of expertise for identifying the evolving exposures in today's global economy.

Our service team has undergone comprehensive training (and has the mandatory regulatory approval) to provide professional services to clients. Our focus is to fulfill the risk and insurance needs of large & mid-sized corporate and public entities.

We offer comprehensive solutions to our clients surpassing traditional insurance services.
